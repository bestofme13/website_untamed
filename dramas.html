The Untamed (Pinyin: Chen Qing Ling) is a 2019 Chinese television series adapted from the xianxia novel Mo Dao Zu Shi by Mo Xiang Tong Xiu. It follows the adventures of two soulmate cultivators who travel to solve a series of mysteries that link to a tragic event in the past.
